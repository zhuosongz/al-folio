Basic information

I am currently a Research Fellow in the Department of Statistics & Applied Probability at the National University of Singapore. My supervisor is Professor Adrian Roellin. Before joining NUS, I was a research fellow under the supervision of Aurore Delaigle at the University of Melbourne. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2017 at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Professor Qi-Man Shao.
