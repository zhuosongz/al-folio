Basic information

I am currently a Research Fellow in the Department of Statistics and Applied Probability at the National University of Singapore working with Professor Adrian Roellin. Prior to this, I was a research fellow working with Aurore Delaigle at the University of Melbourne. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2017 at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Professor Qi-Man Shao.
